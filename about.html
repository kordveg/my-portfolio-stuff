Artist Statement

Comfortably weird and constructing analog and digital homunculi (one pen stroke at a time).

I am a multidisciplinary ATCM artist specializing in character illustration and visual development. My work focuses on drawing stylized, anthropomorphic actors that possess mix human and animal traits. Using elements of black comedy, high-contrast lighting, and raw textures, I build tense, theatrical scenes designed to leave the viewer slightly off-balance.

My creative process is direct and adaptive, and experimental organized chaos. I tend to translate contrasting mental states from high-energy focus to heavy brain fog. My current state of being gets incorporated raw visual assets. I tend to lean into abstraction and expansion of ideas co-occurring rather than telling linear narratives, I also tend to reuse the same cast of characters across different projects to explore themes of isolation and transformation. My workflow varies and bridges from traditional pen-and-ink line-work with digital painting software to externalize inner concepts that are difficult to put into words.

Tools & Competencies

Photoshop / Clip Studio Paint / Procreate / Blender / Maya / Traditional materials, including a variety of pens
