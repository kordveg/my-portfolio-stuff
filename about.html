Artist Statement

Comfortably weird and constructing analog and digital homunculi (one pen stroke at a time).

I am a multidisciplinary ATCM artist specializing in character illustration and visual development. My work focuses on drawing stylized, hybrid creatures that mix human and animal traits. Using elements of black comedy, high-contrast lighting, and raw textures, I build tense, theatrical scenes designed to leave the viewer slightly off-balance.

My creative process is direct and adaptive, translating contrasting mental states—from high-energy focus to heavy brain fog—into raw visual assets. Rather than telling linear narratives, I reuse the same cast of characters across different projects to explore themes of isolation and transformation. My workflow bridges traditional pen-and-ink linework with digital painting software to externalize ideas that are difficult to put into words.

Tools & Competencies

Photoshop / Clip Studio Paint / Procreate / Blender / Maya / Traditional Ink & Graphite
